Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and services

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are based on package weight, dimensions, destination, and delivery speed. Our pricing is transparent with no hidden fees.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and cash payments. Corporate accounts can also be set up for regular customers.

Do you offer discounts for bulk shipments?

Yes, we offer competitive discounts for regular customers and bulk shipments. Contact our sales team for customized pricing.

What is your refund policy?

We offer partial refunds for delayed shipments and full refunds for lost packages (terms and conditions apply).
